Details

This remarkable property at 114 Twisp-Carlton Road is situated in the town of Carlton, WA. It is a quiet, peaceful property with expansive views running south to north.

The land measures 2.5 acres and contains a 2,400-square-foot shop with living quarters and an external bathhouse (built in 2014). The property has irrigation rights and a well, which can accommodate a home plus irrigation. The septic tank is suitable for a 3-bedroom home.

The shop has two driveway entries: one on the north side and one on the south side. The north entrance to the shop is high enough to accommodate a trailer or RV. The living quarters are located above the shop, with an entrance just off the Twisp-Carlton Road, with access through the shop as well, by staircase. The living area contains a bedroom/office (150 square feet) and a larger living space (350 square feet).

Use it for a weekend getaway, with easy access to great fishing and outdoor recreation, or turn it into a full-time home and build from scratch while you live on-site. The shop can be used for commercial purposes, such as manufacturing or storage. The potentials are endless.
