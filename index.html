The property at 114 Twisp-Carlton Road is situated just off the Methow River in the city of Carlton, WA. The property measures 2.5 acres and contains a 1,000 square foot shop with living quarters and an external bathhouse. Property has irrigation rights with a well and a septic tank suitable for a 3-bedroom home. The shop two driveway entries on the north and south side. The north entrance is high enough for a trailer or RV. The shop was previously used for yurt manufacturing. Living quarters are located just off the Twisp-Carlton Road with a bedroom and larger living space. The shop and living quarters are not plumbed. The bathhouse was built in 2014 and contains a shower, toilet, sink, hot water tank, and pressure tank.
